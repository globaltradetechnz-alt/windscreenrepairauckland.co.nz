Windscreen Repair vs Replacement: What Do You Actually Need?

🔵 Just got a small chip?

We only do full replacements. For a small chip, call 0800 202 888 for chip repair specialists.

When Do You Need Replacement?

❌

Large crack (over 20mm)

Requires full replacement for safety.

❌

Crack in driver's line of sight

NZ law requires replacement.

❌

Multiple chips or cracks

Weakens the glass beyond safe repair.

❌

Crack reaching the edge

Edge cracks spread rapidly.
